CWC 2026

September 13-19

Livigno, Italy

Where?

The presentations, accommodation, and meals will take place at the hotel Lac Salin in Livigno, Italy.

This year, we made a special effort to find a hotel that is even easier to access than in previous years. The closest bus stop to the hotel is Livigno, S.Rocco, Pedrana 4, but the hotel is also accessible by a 20 minute walk from the bus stop Livigno, Centro. For connections to Livigno, Centro from Switzerland, please consult the SBB website. For local busses from Italy, please use the Bus Perego website.

By train

From most of the directions, the easiest way of reaching Livigno is via Zürich, from where the connection takes 3 hours. The connections and tickets might be found at the <SBB website. If you are traveling from Germany, it might be cheaper to buy a direct train ticket to Zernez and a bus ticket from there. From Austria, Livigno can also be reached by bus, train and bus again via Landeck and Pfunds. Another cheap option of reaching Livigno from anywhere in Switzerland or from Landeck is buying the SBB Saver Day Pass several month in advance.

By plane

While Livigno is located in Italy, we recommend flying to Zürich as the connections from all Italian airports are long and tedious.

When?

The congress will take place from Monday, 14 September to Friday, 18 September. Arrival is on Sunday evening and departure is on Saturday morning.

How to attend?

Participation is by invitation only. A conference fee will be asked for each participant. If you will not receive an invitation, you can email the organisers.
