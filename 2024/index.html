CWC 2024 PREVIOUS EDITIONS

Where?

Presentations, accommodation, and meals will take place at the Hotel Kolfuschgerhof in Colfosco, Italy.

The hotel is easy to reach by public transport. To find the best connections in South Tyrol, please consult this website.

By train

From the north (Munich, Innsbruck) take a train via Innsbruck and Brenner to Franzensfeste, where you change to a regional train to St. Lorenzen. From there take the bus to Kolfuschg and get off at the last stop, Kolfuschg, Pecëistraße. From there it is a 10 minute walk to the hotel. The last bus leaves St. Lorenzen at 21:27. From the East (Vienna) take a train to Innsbruck and continue as above or to Lienz in Osttirol and from there to Bruneck where you take a bus to Kolfuschg, Pecëistraße. From the south (Italy) either take a train via Bolzano to Franzensfeste and continue as above or take a train via Bolzano to Waidbruck-Lajen, change to a bus to Plan and then take another bus to Kolfuschg, Rönnstraße, which stops directly in front of the hotel.

By plane

The nearest airport is in Bolzano, there are also good connections from Innsbruck (see above). It is also possible to reach the hotel from Verona, the journey takes about five hours, the last train leaves Verona at 17.50. For those who arrive later and cannot make the last train or bus, we can organise a taxi - details will be sent in an email a few weeks before the conference.

When?

The congress will take place from Monday 23 September to Friday 27 September. Arrival is on Sunday, departure on Saturday morning.

How to attend?

As every year, participation is by invitation only. However if you did not receive an invitation by email and wish to participate, you can email the organizers. A conference fee of 140 euros will be asked for each participant.
